About Atlantis

Our Mountain

Our mountain is home to 30 seperate runs ranging from beginner level to expert level. You will never run out of things to do!

Our Team

Our team is comprised of highly skilled ski patrol and instructors who work hard to ensure you have a fun, safe time on the hill!

Rentals

We offer rental equipment for all ages and sizes so everyone can participate in the fun.

Lessons

Our skilled Instructos offer skiing and snowboarding lessons for all ages and skill.

What You Need

When joining us for ski/snowboard lessons you will need:

Warm undergarments (Pants, Shirt, Sweater, Neck Warmer, Socks)
Ski/Snowboard Boots aswell as Skis/Snowboard (Can be Rented)
Helmet (Can be Rented)
Winter Jacket and Snowpants (Can be Rented)
